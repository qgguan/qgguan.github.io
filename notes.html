Research Notes

Technical documents and working notes from my research projects:

  • Weak Galerkin Finite Element Methods: Detailed analysis and implementation notes for WG-FEM on various mesh types.
  • Nonlocal PDEs and Fractional Diffusion: Mathematical foundations and numerical approaches for nonlocal problems.
  • Stochastic PDEs and Uncertainty Quantification: Methods for solving PDEs with random inputs and parameters.
  • Virtual Element Methods: Implementation details and error analysis for VEM.
  • Computational Neuroscience Models: Mathematical models of calcium dynamics in neurons with endoplasmic reticulum.
  • Deep Learning for Scientific Computing: Hybrid approaches combining traditional numerical methods with deep neural networks.
